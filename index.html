About

Browngator is an independent game studio focused on creating immersive, atmospheric experiences driven by strong mechanics and memorable storytelling. Founded with a passion for crafting polished and meaningful games, the studio emphasizes quality, experimentation, and player-first design. BrownGator Games is currently led by its founder, who oversees game design, development, and production, collaborating with artists and specialists when needed to bring each project to life.
